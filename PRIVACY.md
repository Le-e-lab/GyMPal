# 🔏 Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## The Short Version

GyMPal does not collect any of your personal data. Ever. Your workouts, settings, and history live entirely on your own device.

---

## What Data GyMPal Stores

GyMPal stores the following data **locally in your browser**:

| Data | Where | Shared? |
|------|-------|---------|
| Workout history | `localStorage` / `IndexedDB` | Never |
| Custom routines | `localStorage` | Never |
| App settings (theme, preferences) | `localStorage` | Never |
| Body metrics (if logged) | `localStorage` | Never |

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server.

---

## What GyMPal Does NOT Do

- ❌ No user accounts or registration
- ❌ No analytics or tracking scripts
- ❌ No advertising or ad networks
- ❌ No cookies (beyond what the browser uses for localStorage)
- ❌ No third-party data sharing
- ❌ No cloud sync (your data stays on your device)

---

## Third-Party Services

GyMPal is hosted on **GitHub Pages**. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (IP addresses, browser user agents) as part of normal hosting operations. This is outside our control. See [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for details.

If GyMPal uses any external APIs (e.g. an exercise database API), those will be listed here. As of the current version, all exercise data is bundled locally.

---

## Deleting Your Data

To delete all GyMPal data from your browser:

1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)
2. Go to **Application → Storage → Local Storage**
3. Find the GyMPal entry and clear it

Or use your browser's "Clear Site Data" / "Clear Browsing Data" feature for `le-e-lab.github.io`.
